Rule 35

The 35th Rule of Golf reads to have fun!

Golf is for everyone - breaking down the barriers to accessing golf

Fun- sometimes you need to forget the score and enjoy your time with the people you are with. After all...the more fun you have the better you’ll play

Playing with people- don’t be afraid of playing with strangers. It’s about the experience. Brotherhood, sisterhood, and making new friends
